# Bookshelf and Reading Log Conventions

The Bookshelf page is Reagan's reading record. Every book she reads — out loud, silent, or read-to — gets a row.

## States
- **Reading** — currently in the middle of it.
- **Finished** — completed; counts toward the IH portfolio reading log.
- **Wishlist** — Mom or Reagan flagged it for later.

## Tags we standardize on
- `fiction` / `nonfiction`
- `reagan-pick` (she chose it) / `assigned` (curriculum)
- `audiobook` (still counts!)
- `novel-in-verse`, `graphic-novel`, `chapter-book`, `picture-book`
- `series-name:WingsOfFire` (or whichever)

## Daily reading logging
The Day Log auto-fills time spent reading from any `reading` block + any Bookshelf "session" entries. You don't double-count.

## Year-end export
Run "Export Reading Log" on the Bookshelf page; it produces a CSV + Markdown summary in this folder, ready for the IH portfolio.
