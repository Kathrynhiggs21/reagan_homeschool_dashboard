## How to use this packet

1. Open `03_Assignment_Tracker.csv` in Google Sheets. Move tasks from **To-Do → Doing → Done** as Reagan works through them.
2. Submit completed work by uploading a photo, typed doc, or voice note to the adult analytics dashboard.
3. Check off items in `02_5th_Grade_Curriculum_Scope_and_Sequence.md` as topics are mastered.
4. Review `05_Parent_Tutor_Handoff.md` with anyone new supporting her.
5. Revisit `01_Academic_Snapshot_and_IEP_Goals.md` every 6 weeks to update baseline numbers.
