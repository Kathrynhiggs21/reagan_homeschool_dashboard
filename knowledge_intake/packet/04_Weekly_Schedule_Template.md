# Reagan's Homeschool Weekly Schedule
**Start:** Spring 2026 | **Approach:** Short focused blocks with breaks, interest-led extensions, and generous movement.

## Daily Rhythm (suggested)

| Time | Block | What Happens |
|---|---|---|
| 9:00 – 9:15 | Warm-Up & Check-In | Emotional check-in (1-10), review the day's plan, pick reward. |
| 9:15 – 9:45 | Math (core) | Fluency 10 min + concept 20 min. Use manipulatives. |
| 9:45 – 10:00 | Movement / Snack | Stretch, walk Dutch, or dance break. |
| 10:00 – 10:30 | Reading | Fluency passage + comprehension task. |
| 10:30 – 10:45 | Movement / Break | Outside if weather allows. |
| 10:45 – 11:15 | Writing | Graphic organizer + 1 paragraph or spelling. |
| 11:15 – 12:00 | Lunch & Free Time | Roblox or Pinterest allowed as reward. |
| 12:00 – 12:30 | Science or Social Studies (alternate days) | One hands-on activity or video + response. |
| 12:30 – 1:00 | Wrap-Up / Submit | Photograph completed work, upload to analytics dashboard, review. |

## Weekly Rotation

| Day | Science/SS Focus | Special Activity |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Science | Crystals / experiment |
| Tuesday | Social Studies | BrainPOP + chart |
| Wednesday | Science | Bird watching outing |
| Thursday | Social Studies | iCivics game |
| Friday | Review & Fun Friday | Art integration, field trip, or cooking (math + science) |

## Built-In Supports

- **Calming tools available at all times:** fidget, noise-cancelling headphones, stuffed animal.
- **Emergency reset:** 5 deep breaths, walk Dutch, drink water, try again.
- **Reward system:** Complete 3 of 4 morning blocks = 30 min Roblox / Pinterest at lunch.
- **Submission:** Every task ends with a photo or Google Doc shared to the adult analytics dashboard (no Google Classroom).
