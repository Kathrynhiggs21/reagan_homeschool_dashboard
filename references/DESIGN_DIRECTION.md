# Reagan School — Canonical Theme Direction (Katy, 2026-07-01)

> **Voice check:** NOT whimsical, NOT magical, NOT glittery, NOT cartoonish.
> The words Katy uses: **clear, beautiful, minimal, 3D glass, realistic.**

## General theme prompt (clear · minimal · 3D glass)

A clean, minimal, modern homeschool website built on **clear 3D liquid glass**.
Realistic, **photorealistic nature backgrounds** (soft-focus forest, sunny meadow,
or calm dusk) sit full-bleed behind the interface, with **two real budgies — one
yellow, one blue-and-green — perched on a branch** as a subtle natural accent,
**never over text**. All UI is genuine clear 3D glass: truly transparent,
backdrop-blurred panels with **crisp beveled light-rims, a soft realistic drop
shadow, and a subtle specular highlight**, so each surface reads as a real pane of
glass floating above the scene. Generous white space, restrained **jewel-tone
accents (teal, periwinkle, gold, sky-blue)**, clean modern **sans-serif**
typography, and simple rounded glass buttons. Calm, clear, premium, minimal —
dimensional glass, not flat cards, not busy, not cartoonish, not glittery.

## Global layout

- **Slim translucent glass top bar:** "Reagan School" name/avatar left; weather +
  parent-access lock right.
- **Primary nav = floating glass dock of rounded jewel "gem" icons**
  (Home, Today, Schedule, Kiwi, Books, Apps) — bottom-centered on mobile,
  dockable left/right on desktop; **active icon lifts and glows**.
- **Content scrolls in a single centered column** with comfortable margins.
- Nothing fills more than it needs; no empty dead space, no clutter.

## Per-page intent

- **Landing:** Full-bleed scene; slim time-aware greeting top-left; centered band
  of six clear glass gem tiles (icon + bold label + tiny subtitle); a small live
  "Kiwi Coins" glass chip and one calm encouragement line near the bottom.
- **Today:** Wide glass hero (greeting, date, compact subject-progress ring
  cluster); below, the day flows as clean floating glass time-rows
  (time · subject color · title · quick actions). No heavy list bricks, no empty
  placeholder circles.
- **Schedule:** Glass week view — translucent day columns with soft-colored glass
  event chips; today's column gently highlighted.
- **Books / Apps:** Calm responsive grid of clear glass cards (cover/icon, title,
  one action); consistent radius, spacing, shadow.
- **Kiwi / Coins:** Glass chat panel + glass coin-balance card with a simple
  progress bar and redeem buttons.
- **Practice / Progress:** Glass panels with clean 3D progress rings and minimal
  charts; restrained color, lots of breathing room.

## Rules (hard constraints)

1. Real transparency + backdrop blur on **every** surface.
2. Text is **always high-contrast and legible over glass** (add a readability
   scrim behind text where the scene is busy).
3. Birds and scene are **decorative only** — never behind or over reading text.
4. Consistent **16–20px radius**, **soft shadows over borders**, one accent color
   per section.
5. **Sans-serif** body/UI. (Earlier "soft serif" note is overridden by Katy's
   2026-07-01 voice check: clean modern sans-serif.)

## One-liner

Clear, minimal 3D liquid-glass UI over realistic nature — transparent beveled
panels, soft real shadows, restrained jewel accents, clean sans-serif, a floating
gem dock, and calm uncluttered page layouts.
