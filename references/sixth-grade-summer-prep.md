# 6th Grade Summer Prep — Reagan

**Purpose:** After 5th grade curriculum topics are marked complete, these summer bridge assignments
queue up automatically in the agenda editor. The goal is to preview key 6th grade concepts so Reagan
starts the year with confidence rather than cold-starting on new material.

---

## When This Activates

The agenda AI checks this list when:
1. The current date is on or after **June 1** (summer break start)
2. At least **80% of 5th grade curriculum topics** are marked complete in the Curriculum Hub
3. The block type is `choice`, `catch_up`, or `custom` with no assigned topic

When all three conditions are met, the AI may suggest a summer prep block from the list below.

## Summer Reading List

These books bridge 5th→6th grade reading levels and connect to curriculum topics:

- **The Giver** by Lois Lowry (dystopia, social studies connection)
- **Hatchet** by Gary Paulsen (survival, science connection)
- **Wonder** by R.J. Palacio (ELA, character study)
- **National Geographic Kids: Ancient Egypt** (social studies bridge)
- **The One and Only Ivan** by Katherine Applegate (ELA, animal science)

## AI Agenda Integration Notes

- Suggest a Kiwi quiz on any topic covered that week
- Flag topics as "6th grade preview" in the block description so it's clear to Reagan and Mom
