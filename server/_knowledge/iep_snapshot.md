## 2. IEP Goals & Accommodations (Actionable for Homeschool)

### Goal 1: Reading Fluency & Decoding
- **Target:** Read 110 correct words per minute with 97% accuracy.
- **Homeschool Action:** Practice decoding multisyllable words with consonant-le patterns (candle, middle, bugle). Use repeated readings of passages and multisensory phonics.

### Goal 2: Written Expression
- **Target:** Score 7+ out of 8 on a paragraph writing rubric (Organization, Evidence, Language, Conventions).
- **Homeschool Action:** ALWAYS provide graphic organizers. Focus on getting ideas down first, mechanics second. Provide explicit spelling instruction for vowel teams and r-controlled vowels. Allow alternative formats (oral, visual, speech-to-text).

### Goal 3: Math Problem Solving & Computation
- **Target:** 80% accuracy on two-step word problems involving all 4 operations.
- **Homeschool Action:** Practice single-digit multiplication/division fluency. Use visual representations, manipulatives, hundreds charts, and number lines. Explicitly teach how to select the correct operation in word problems.

### Critical Accommodations (Non-Negotiable)
- **Testing:** Small group/1-on-1 setting, extended time (up to 50%), frequent breaks, test materials read aloud (except reading tests).
- **Instruction:** Break activities into smaller segments, flexible deadlines, reduced quantity (not quality), access to math tools (multiplication chart, number line).
- **Emotional Support:** Flexible attendance/responses, break opportunities without penalty, private correction, celebrate growth over perfection.
